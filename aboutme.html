10/16/20

It feels strange to scrap something I've invested so much into, but it's come time to say goodbye to the lightmode toggle :(. For my sake, the feature deserves a eulogy and an explanation.

I originally implemented it to give the site an alternate, less eye straining, display method. The website was paper white by default because the home page was supposed to be a comic page, but over time this became less practical.

Every font color had to display nicely on both background colors; the blinding flash of white as the page rendered meant that dark mode had to be changed to be on by default; every page had to have the toggle button, with its custom font. The nav element containing the toggle button lost most of its children and ended up existing only to hold that one button. The scripting turned the small task of adjusting color styles a much more labor intensive task.

It was prohibitive in a lot of ways, and it's time for it to be scrapped. While a fun feature to think about, unfortunately it's no longer needed.
