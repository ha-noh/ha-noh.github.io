Hello, I'm Andrew - I do web developer and digital illustrator things. You can find my various professional and hobby projects on this site! There's not much else to say at the moment, so here's an exhuastive history of this site's improvements over time:

  • 10/16/20

    It feels strange to scrap an evergreen feature, but it's time to say goodbye to the lightmode toggle :(.

    Lightmode's, originally nightmode's, purpose was to provide a less eye-straining display method. It was only really necessary because I thought the website's background had to be white; you know, because the home page is supposed to be a comic book page. So really, having a toggle was a compromise between thematics and comfort.

    Having two modes for every page brought twice the issues: Every font color had to display nicely on both background colors; the implementation of the nightmode switch made the site jarringly flash white on every page load; adding colors to the palette was more labor-intensive than it should have been; every page had to have the nav element containing the toggle button. This element even lost most its purpose over time, and eventually only existed to hold the toggle button itself.

    The feature is more prohibitive than useful, and it's time for it to be scrapped. While it was fun to think about, unfortunately it's no longer needed. Thanks for coming to my TEDx talk.


  • 12/5/20

    You can now reach any of the site's pages, from any page, with one click (two for mobile). The new navbar makes the site easier to navigate.
